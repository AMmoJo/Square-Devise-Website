Square Devise (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Android app.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected by Google Mobile Ads SDK
We do not collect personal data ourselves; however, the Google Mobile Ads SDK automatically collects the following when you use the app:

  • Advertising and device identifiers
  • IP address
  • User interactions
  • Diagnostic information

All data transmitted by the SDK is encrypted in transit via TLS.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected by AdMob’s SDK is used by Google and us to:

  • Serve personalized ads based on your interests.
  • Measure ad performance and app usage analytics.
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues within the SDK.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share data ourselves; however, Google (and its advertising partners) may share this information among themselves to deliver and optimize ads. For details, see Google’s Privacy & Terms pages.

4. Your Choices

  • Opt-out of personalized ads: You can reset or opt out of your Advertising ID in your device’s settings.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: AdMob may drop cookies or use similar local storage for ad delivery.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy; the “Effective date” above will reflect the last revision.
